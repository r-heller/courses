# decision-curves

Companion to **Course 2, Week 4, Session 4 — Decision curves, NRI/IDI**
(also relevant to Course 4, Week 3, Session 5). Pick prevalence and the
AUC of two competing prediction models. The app simulates predicted
probabilities under the binormal model and plots the decision curves
of: baseline model, new model, treat-all, treat-none.

Packages: `shiny`, `ggplot2`, `tidyr`.
