# missing-data-sim

Companion to **Course 3, Week 2, Session 1 — MCAR / MAR / MNAR.**
Simulate Y as correlated with X, drop part of Y under a chosen
missingness mechanism, and compare the complete-case mean of Y with
the true mean across replications. The bias under MAR/MNAR is
visible at a glance — and disappears under MCAR.

Packages: `shiny`, `ggplot2`.
