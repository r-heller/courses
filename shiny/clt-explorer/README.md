# clt-explorer

Interactive companion to **Course 1, Week 3, Session 1 — Populations,
samples, and the central limit theorem.** Pick a parent distribution
(uniform, exponential, lognormal, Bernoulli), pick a sample size, and
watch the sampling distribution of the mean tighten and turn Gaussian
as *n* grows.

Packages: `shiny`, `ggplot2`.
